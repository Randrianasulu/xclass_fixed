Keyboard shortcuts

<Right-Arrow>Tune Up
<Left-Arrow>Tune Down
<PgUp>Tune Up by 10 times the tuning step
<PgDn>Tune Down by 10 times the tuning step
<Up-Arrow>Increase Main Volume
<Down-Arrow>Decrease Main Volume
<Shift-Up-Arrow>Increase Line-Out Volume
<Shift-Down-Arrow>Decrease Line-Out Volume
A or <Alt-A>AM Mode
U or <Alt-U>USB Mode
L or <Alt-L>LSB Mode
C or <Alt-C>CW Mode
+Increase tuning step
-Decrease tuning step
0 - 9Begin Entering Frequency
<Enter>Finish Entering Frequency
<Escape>Abort Entering Frequency
<Space>Switch VFOs
<Alt-S>Slow AGC
<Alt-M>Medium AGC
<Alt-F>Fast AGC
M or <Alt-t>Toggle Mute
<Alt-X>Exit
<Alt-D>Open a Frequency Database Window
<F1>Help
